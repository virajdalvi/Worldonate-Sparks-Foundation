About Us

Children in poverty make up thirty-nine percent of the population, and most of these children do not receive a good education because their parents cannot afford to buy the material required for their studies.

Worldonate being a 10-year-old NGO helps underprivileged students by providing them with educational materials such as books, notebooks, e-tabs, compass boxes, etc. We also organized the education awareness drive among the children to make them realize the importance of education. With our volunteers, we had helped 1000+ students.
